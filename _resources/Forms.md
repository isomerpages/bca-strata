<ol>
<li>
<p>Please ensure that handwritten forms are neat and legible, or the form
may not be accepted for processing.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If you are filling out a form in MS Word format, please ensure that the
finished document conforms to the required format as shown in the PDF version.
Fields should not be removed from a MS Word version form.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Hardcopies of signed and completed forms are to be submitted to the Strata
Titles Boards.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>No legal advice can be given.</p>
</li>
</ol>
